Go To - Tips

1. Toggle between Go to Everything and Go to Type by repeatedly
   hitting the shortcut

2. Switching between Go to Everything, type and symbol while
   the popup is displayed maintains the search text
   Type "Go" and switch between the go to popups

3. Hold down Ctrl to make the popup transparent

4. Click the magnifying glass icon in the top right of the popup,
   or press the plus key on the numpad (doesn't work on laptop)
   to display the results of any popup in the Find Results window
